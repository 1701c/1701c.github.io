About Me

While I'm originally from Brooklyn, New York, I recently moved to Austin Texas. Before moving, I spent 15 years as the IT Director of an Equity Management firm in Rockefeller Center. Keeping with the theme of change, I have also decided to pickup some new skills. As anyone in Austin may tell you, the opportunities for web developers are great, and I've never been opposed to learning something new.

I spend most of my free time tinkering with old computer hardware, hacking old Nintendo consoles to work with my modern HD TV, and being generally disappointed in the performance of the New York Mets.
